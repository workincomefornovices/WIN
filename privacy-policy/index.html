Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how WIN (Work Income for Novices) and its affiliates (collectively "WIN", "we", "our", "us") collect, use, share, protect, and process your personal data through our website https://www.workincomefornovices.com/ (the "Platform").

By visiting this Platform, providing your information, or availing any of our digital agency services or training registrations, you expressly agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and the applicable Terms of Use. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use or access our Platform.

2. Information Collection

We collect your personal data when you use our Platform, engage with our digital services (such as web development, SEO, graphic design), or register for our training programs. The information we may collect includes, but is not limited to:

  • Contact Data: Name, address, telephone/mobile number, email ID, and business details provided during sign-up/inquiry.
  • Financial Data: Bank account, credit/debit card, or other payment instrument information processed securely via our payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, PhonePe) during transactions.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and interaction behaviour mapped on our Platform (compiled and analysed on an aggregated basis).

3. Usage of Personal Data

We use your personal data to ensure smooth service delivery and operational efficiency. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Provide, fulfill, and manage your service requests and training program registrations.
  • Facilitate transactions and process payments securely.
  • Communicate with you regarding project updates, invoices, offline/online offers, and support inquiries.
  • Enhance user experience and customize our Platform according to your preferences.
  • Detect and prevent error, fraud, unauthorized access, and other criminal or illegal activities.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We restrict the sharing of your personal data. We only share information under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (such as payment gateways, hosting partners, email providers) obligated to keep your data secure and only use it to provide specific services to WIN.
  • Legal Compliance: With law enforcement agencies or authorized government bodies if required to do so by law, in good faith to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, where data transfer is necessary for business continuity.

5. Security Precautions

To protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, or misuse, we adopt industry-standard security practices. All sensitive payment transactions are routed securely through authorized payment gateway partners. While we offer a secure server environment, we acknowledge that data transmission over the internet cannot be fully guaranteed as immune to breaches. Users are responsible for safeguarding any login credentials assigned to their accounts.

6. Data Deletion and Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable law (such as financial or tax regulations). If you wish to delete your account or data, you may submit a request to our Grievance Officer. However, we may delay or refuse deletion in cases of active projects, pending grievances, or regulatory obligations.

7. Your Rights and Consent

By providing your information, you consent to its collection and processing as described. You reserve the right to access, rectify, or update your personal data. You may withdraw your consent at any time by writing to our Grievance Officer, with the subject line “Withdrawal of consent for processing personal data”. Please note that withdrawing consent will not act retrospectively and may restrict our ability to provide you with certain services.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our operational or legal obligations. We recommend reviewing this page frequently for the latest information on our privacy practices.
