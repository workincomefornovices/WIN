Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Policy Overview

This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product or service that you have purchased through the WIN (Work Income for Novices) Platform. Because our offerings range from personalized digital agency services to structured training registrations, our policy is categorised to ensure fairness and transparency.

2. Cancellation Timelines

A. Training Program Registrations

Cancellations for training programs, webinars, or online courses will be considered if the request is made within 7 days of placing the order, provided the batch or session has not officially commenced. Cancellation requests cannot be entertained if the user has already received access to proprietary curriculum material or if the training session has already begun.

B. Digital Agency Services

For custom services such as Web Development, Digital Marketing, SEO Strategy, and Graphic Design, cancellations must be requested before the commencement of the specific project phase. WIN does not accept cancellation requests for:

  • Personalized digital assets or graphics that have already been designed and approved.
  • Development milestones that have been coded, deployed, or delivered.
  • Hosting, domain registrations, or third-party software licenses procured on your behalf.

3. Conditions for Refund or Replacement

In all circumstances, we strive for exceptional quality. A refund or replacement of services can be invoked under the following conditions:

  • Defective Deliverables: If you establish that the digital product, code, or design asset explicitly fails to meet the agreed-upon Statement of Work (SOW) or project scope.
  • Service Unavailability: If a training program you paid for is permanently cancelled by us without a rescheduled date.

If you feel that the provided service or product is not as shown on the site or as per your documented expectations, you must bring it to the notice of our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the deliverable. Our team, after reviewing your complaint against the project scope, will take an appropriate decision.

4. Warranties and Long-term Support

In case of complaints regarding developmental products or software structures that come with a specific warranty or Service Level Agreement (SLA), please refer to the specific clauses outlined in your individual project contract. Such SLA issues are handled independently from this general consumer refund policy.

5. Refund Processing Time

In case of any refunds approved by WIN (Work Income for Novices), it will take 5 to 7 business days for the refund to be fully processed. The refunded amount will be credited back to your original source of payment (e.g., Credit Card, Bank Account, UPI) within 5 to 7 Business days.
